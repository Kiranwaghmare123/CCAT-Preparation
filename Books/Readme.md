

#  **C-CAT Subject-Wise Book List (Best + Standard + Optional References)**


#  **SECTION A — Aptitude, English, Computer Fundamentals**

---

## **1. English**

### **Primary Books**

1. **High School English Grammar & Composition – Wren & Martin**
2. **Objective General English – S.P. Bakshi (Arihant)** *(optional but good for practice)*

### **Why these?**

* Covers grammar, vocabulary, comprehension like exam pattern.

---

## **2. Quantitative Aptitude**

### **Primary Books**

1. **Quantitative Aptitude – R.S. Aggarwal**
2. **Magical Book on Quicker Maths – M Tyra**

### **Advanced Practice (Optional)**

3. **Barron’s New GRE (Quant sections)**

### **Why these?**

* Perfect for time-work, ratio, speed–distance and arithmetic-based questions.

---

## **3. Reasoning / Logical Aptitude**

### **Primary Books**

1. **A Modern Approach to Logical Reasoning – R.S. Aggarwal**
2. **Analytical Reasoning – M.K. Pandey** *(optional)*

---

## **4. Computer Fundamentals**

### **Primary Book**

1. **Foundations of Computing – Pradeep K. Sinha & Priti Sinha**

### **Why this?**

* Covers OS basics, computer architecture basics, flowcharts, number systems.

---

## **5. Concepts of Programming**

### **Primary Book**

1. **Foundations of Computing – Sinha & Sinha (same book covers this too)**

---
