#  **What is C-CAT?**

**C-CAT (Common Admission Test)** is the **entrance exam conducted by C-DAC ACTS** for admission into various **6-month PG Diploma courses** offered across C-DAC centres in India.

Every candidate must clear C-CAT and obtain a good rank to get admission into the centre/course of their choice.

---

#  **Purpose of the C-CAT Exam**

* To assess the candidate’s **aptitude, reasoning, and technical knowledge**.
* To **allocate ranks**.
* Seat allocation depends on **C-CAT rank + preference order** given by the student.

---

#  **C-CAT Exam Structure**

C-CAT contains **three sections**, but **you attempt only the sections relevant to your course choices**.

## **Section A — Mandatory for All Courses**

| Topic                 | Includes                      |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| English               | Grammar, Comprehension        |
| Quantitative Aptitude | Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry |
| Reasoning             | Analytical reasoning, Puzzles |

---

## **Section B — For Technical Courses**

(Required for courses like PG-DAC, PG-DBDA, PG-AI, PG-IoT, PG-DESD, etc.)

Topics:

* C Programming
* Data Structures
* OOP Concepts
* Computer Architecture
* Operating Systems

---

## **Section C — For Advanced Specializations**

(Used for specific courses like PG-DAI, PG-VLSI, PG-ESD, etc.)

Topics:

* Digital Electronics
* Microprocessors
* Computer Networks

Each section has:

* **50 Questions**
* **150 Marks**
* **Negative marking**: –1 for wrong answer, +3 for correct answer

---

#  **C-CAT Exam Mode**

* **Online (CBT – Computer Based Test)**
* Conducted across multiple centres in India
* Duration: **90 minutes per section**

---

#  **Eligibility**

Eligibility varies by course, but generally:

* Engineering graduates (BE/BTech – ANY branch)
* MCA, MSc (IT/CS/Electronics)
* BSc (IT/CS) + experience (for some courses)
* Final-year students can apply (provisional admission)

---

#  **When Is C-CAT Conducted?**

C-DAC conducts admissions **twice a year**:

| Batch               | Exam Time   | Course Start |
| ------------------- | ----------- | ------------ |
| **March Batch**     | January     | Fenruary     |
| **September Batch** | July        | August       |

---

---

#  **Ranking & Seat Allocation**

* After the exam, C-DAC publishes **rank lists**.
* You fill preferences for **courses + centres**.
* Seats are allotted based on:

  1. Your **C-CAT rank**
  2. Your **preference order**
  3. Seat availability
